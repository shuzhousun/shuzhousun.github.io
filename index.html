Biography

I am a final-year PhD candidate at Machine Vision and Signal Analysis (CMVS) in University of Oulu, Finland, supervised by Prof. Li Liu and Prof. Janne Heikkilä. I have also completed three visiting research experiences: at Tsinghua University, Beijing, China (Mar 2024 – Aug 2024), supervised by Prof. Jun Zhu; at Nankai University, Tianjin, China (Oct 2024), supervised by Prof. Ming-Ming Cheng; and at Shanghai AI Lab, China (Jun 2025 – Aug 2025), supervised by Prof. Wanli Ouyang. My recent research interests is AI for Science.
