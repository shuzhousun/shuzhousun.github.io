Biography

I am a second-year PhD candidate at Machine Vision and Signal Analysis (CMVS) in University of Oulu, Finland, supervised by Prof. Li Liu and Prof. Janne Heikkilä. Before that, I got my B.Eng degree from Henan Agricultural University in 2018, and received MSc.Eng degree from Shanghai Normal University in 2022. My research interests include deep learning, computer vision, and causal inference.
